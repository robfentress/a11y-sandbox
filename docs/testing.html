Report a barrier

Virginia Tech is committed to ensuring equal access and strives to maintain a barrier-free environment.

Physical barrier

Physical barriers on campus may include items such as an inoperative elevator or chair lift, a blocked pathway, or an inaccessible entrance. To report a physical barrier at Virginia Tech, fill out the online form.
